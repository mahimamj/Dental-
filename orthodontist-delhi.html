FAQs

Common Questions About Visiting an Orthodontist in Delhi

When should I visit an orthodontist instead of a general dentist?

Visit an orthodontist when you need teeth straightening, bite correction, jaw alignment assessment, or braces and aligner treatment planning.

Can adults get orthodontic treatment in Delhi?

Yes. Many adult patients choose Invisalign, ceramic braces, or other discreet treatment plans based on their bite and lifestyle goals.

How long does teeth straightening take?

Teeth straightening time depends on how mild or complex the case is. A consultation is the best way to estimate your treatment timeline accurately.

Do I need braces or Invisalign?

That depends on the severity of alignment and bite issues, your preferences, and how much control the case requires.

Can an orthodontist help with overbite and jaw alignment?

Yes. Orthodontists diagnose and treat overbite, bite imbalance, and jaw alignment issues with braces, aligners, and bite-focused planning.
