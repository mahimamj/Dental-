FAQs

Common Questions About Invisalign Treatment in Delhi

Is Invisalign better than braces?

Invisalign is better for some patients and braces are better for others. The right choice depends on case complexity, bite correction needs, and lifestyle preferences.

How long does Invisalign take?

Invisalign treatment time depends on how much movement is needed. Mild cases can finish faster than comprehensive bite corrections.

Are clear aligners noticeable?

Clear aligners are designed to be discreet and are much less noticeable than fixed braces for most adults and teens.

Who is eligible for Invisalign?

Eligibility depends on your alignment issue, bite relationship, and whether your case can be controlled predictably with aligners.

Can Invisalign fix teeth gaps and crowding?

Yes, Invisalign can help many spacing and crowding cases, but suitability still depends on the severity of the problem.
