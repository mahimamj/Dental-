Common Adult Concerns

What Adults Usually Want to Know Before Starting

Will treatment be visible?

Adults usually compare Invisalign, ceramic braces, and lingual braces depending on social and professional needs.

Will treatment affect work life?

We plan around meetings, travel, hygiene habits, and how much appliance visibility matters to you.

Can old gaps or crowding return?

Yes, relapse can happen. That is why retention planning matters as much as active alignment.
