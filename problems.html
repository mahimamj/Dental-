Problem 01

Teeth Gap Treatment in Delhi

Teeth gaps can be caused by tooth size differences, habits, missing teeth, or bite patterns. The right treatment may involve braces, Invisalign, or a wider interdisciplinary plan depending on the cause.

Treatment Options

For mild to moderate spacing, clear aligners can work well. For more complex bite-related gaps, fixed braces may offer stronger control.

Best Next Step

Start with a consultation if you want to know whether a gap can be closed with aligners alone.

Problem 02

Crowded Teeth Treatment in Delhi

Crowding affects smile appearance, hygiene access, and sometimes bite comfort. Treatment planning depends on severity, arch space, profile considerations, and whether the case needs braces or aligners.

How We Evaluate It

We assess overlap, rotation, bite interference, and whether space can be created without compromising stability.

Best Next Step

Moderate to severe crowding often needs stronger mechanics, while mild crowding may work with aligners.

See Braces for Crowding

Problem 03

Overbite Treatment in Delhi

Overbite treatment is about more than straight front teeth. It can affect smile display, wear patterns, lip posture, and functional comfort, which is why diagnosis by an orthodontist matters.

Typical Solutions

Treatment may include braces, aligners, elastics, and staged bite correction mechanics depending on your bite relationship.

Best Next Step

If you suspect your bite is deep or imbalanced, book a specialist orthodontic visit instead of relying on cosmetic comparisons alone.

See Orthodontic Assessment

Problem 04

Jaw Alignment Treatment in Delhi

Jaw alignment treatment can involve growth guidance, braces, aligners, or more advanced planning depending on the age of the patient and the severity of the discrepancy.

Why Specialist Evaluation Matters

Jaw issues often need a different level of planning than simple cosmetic straightening, especially when function and facial balance are involved.

Best Next Step

FAQs

Common Questions About Orthodontic Problems and Teeth Straightening

Can teeth gaps close without braces?

Some small gaps may be treated with aligners or other options, but many spacing problems still need orthodontic correction for predictable closure.

How do you fix an overbite?

Overbite treatment usually depends on the severity of the bite issue and may involve braces, aligners, elastics, or other bite correction planning.

What causes crowded teeth?

Crowded teeth can be caused by limited jaw space, eruption patterns, inherited tooth-jaw size differences, or relapse after earlier treatment.

Can jaw alignment problems be treated with braces or aligners?

Many jaw alignment problems can be improved with orthodontic treatment, but the exact plan depends on age, growth pattern, and severity.

Which treatment is better for gaps and crowding: braces or aligners?

Both braces and aligners can treat gaps and crowding. The best option depends on how much control your case needs and whether bite correction is also required.
