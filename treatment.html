Treatment Guide

Dental Treatments Available at Star Orthodontist Clinic

Learn about the major treatment options available at the clinic, including root canal treatment, braces, implants, teeth whitening, dentures, crowns, bridges, and wisdom teeth care.

Book Consultation WhatsApp Us

Treatment 01

Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment helps save an infected or painful natural tooth by cleaning the inner canal space, disinfecting it, and sealing it to reduce reinfection risk.

Common Reasons

  • Deep decay or tooth infection
  • Persistent tooth pain or sensitivity
  • Tooth damage that reaches the nerve

Treatment 02

Dental Braces

Dental braces correct teeth alignment, spacing, crowding, and bite problems using fixed appliances planned around the patient's tooth position and jaw relationship.

Useful For

  • Crooked or crowded teeth
  • Spacing between teeth
  • Bite correction and smile balance

Treatment 03

Dental Implant

A dental implant replaces a missing tooth with a stable tooth-root substitute and a custom crown, helping restore chewing function and natural smile appearance.

Planning Includes

  • Bone and gum assessment
  • Implant placement planning
  • Crown shade and bite matching

Treatment 04

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening helps reduce visible stains and brighten the smile through professionally guided whitening options selected after checking tooth and gum suitability.

Best For

  • Surface staining
  • Dull or yellow-looking teeth
  • Smile brightening before events

Treatment 05

Dentures

Dentures replace missing teeth with removable or fixed prosthetic options designed to improve chewing, speech support, facial profile, and day-to-day confidence.

Options May Include

  • Partial dentures
  • Complete dentures
  • Fixed denture solutions where suitable

Treatment 06

Metal Free Crown

Metal free crowns are tooth-coloured restorations used to protect and rebuild damaged teeth while maintaining a more natural-looking smile appearance.

Often Chosen For

  • Front tooth aesthetics
  • Natural tooth-coloured appearance
  • Restoring strength after treatment

Treatment 07

Wisdom Teeth

Wisdom teeth assessment helps identify pain, swelling, decay, infection, or impaction around third molars and decide whether monitoring or extraction is needed.

When to Visit

  • Pain at the back of the jaw
  • Swelling or repeated gum infection
  • Food trapping around wisdom teeth

Treatment 08

Tooth Crowns & Bridges

Tooth crowns restore damaged teeth, while dental bridges replace missing teeth by using neighboring teeth or planned supports to hold the replacement in place.

Used For

  • Broken or weakened teeth
  • Missing tooth replacement
  • Improving bite support and appearance
