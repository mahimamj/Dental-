FAQs

Common Questions About Braces Treatment in Delhi

What is the cost of braces in Delhi?

The cost of braces in Delhi depends on the type of braces, case complexity, treatment duration, and retention needs after treatment.

Which type of braces is best?

The best type of braces depends on your bite correction needs, appearance preference, comfort expectations, and treatment complexity.

Are braces painful?

Braces can feel sore after placement or adjustments, but most patients describe it as temporary pressure that settles in a few days.

Can adults get braces?

Yes. Adult braces are common and can be combined with aesthetic options depending on case suitability.

How long does braces treatment take?

Treatment time varies by case severity, bite correction goals, and how much movement is required to create a stable result.
